Description: About Us

Welcome to Syracuse student community!

This online tool is here to help you!

Why?

If you are a part time employee at any of SU Dining and Food Services, then this tool enables you to gain access to relevant information and facilitates time management.

How?

By logging in to your profile, you have access to information related to shifts available, for you to to grab it up. Also, it gives you special features like pick, drop shifts,complaint log system,payroll management.

Objectives:
  • Internal Record keeping
  • Time and attendance tracking
  • Payroll management
  • Streamline dining process
  • Reduce hassle
  • Make part time jobs more accessible
